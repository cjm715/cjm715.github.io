Christopher J. Miles

My research includes optimal fluid mixing (How should one stir to optimally mix a concentration given a fixed energy budget?), acoustic droplet vaporization (What ultrasonic waveforms will cause micron-sized droplets to vaporize?), and confined active soft matter (What are the dynamics of a droplet containing active matter?).
